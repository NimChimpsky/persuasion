# Game title
- murder at the olive farm

# Intro
- A body is found at an olive farm on a Greek island. The farm is owned by Mrs Giakoumakis and her son Giannis lives there. Carissa is Giannis's ex-girlfriend. You are the detective who must identify the killer.

# Plot points
- An unidentified male body is found at the olive farm.
- An autopsy reveals cocaine and heroin in the dead body.
- Interpol fingerprint search identifies the body as Albanian gang member Hysni Erjet.
- Giannis Giakoumakis had recently been seen arguing with Hysni Erjet.
- Carissa witnessed the argument.
