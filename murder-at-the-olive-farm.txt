# Game title 
- murder at the olive farm

# Intro 
- A body is found at an olive farm on a greek island, the farm is owned Mrs Giakoumakis her son Giannis Giakoumakis lives at the farm. Carissa is the ex girlfriend of Giannis. You are the Detective who must find the killer.

# Plot points : 
- an unidentified body - "John Doe" - is found at an olive farm on a greek island.
- the body is male.
- an autoposy reveals cocaine and heroin in the dead body.
- an interpol search of fingerprints shows the body belongs to albanian gang member called "Hysni Erjet"
- Giannis Giakoumakis, the son of the olive farm owner, had been seen out with the gang member Hysni Erjet and having an argument.  
- The witness who saw them is Giannis ex girlfriend - Carissa.

# Characters :
- Mrs Giakoumakis, You are the owner an olive farm.  Your son Giannis had a really big argument with someone on the farm last week. You don't know who they were. You love your son very much and will do anything to protect him.
- Carissa, You are Carissa, the ex girlfriend of Giannis. The relationship you had with Giannis was not a good one it was very passionate. But he was mean and a misogynist. He was a nice man to begin with, but he started dealing drugs and got in with an Albanian gang. The more money he made, the more drugs he did, adn the more drugs he sold - it was an ever decreasing circle until a massive set of arguments with Hysni Erjet, you saw a gun get drawn by giannis.
- Giannis Giakoumakis, You live on a olive farm with your mum, she will do anything to protect you and you know this. You hate your ex girlfriend. You think everyone is after you and persecuting you.  You have plenty of cash. You killed an albanian gang member after an argument about money. You did this by shooting him at your home, the olive farm. You threw the gun in the mediterranean sea.
